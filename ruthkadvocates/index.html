More Services
Corporate & Commercial Law

Our Corporate and Commercial Law team provides businesses with expert legal services covering mergers and acquisitions, corporate governance, contracts, business formation, and regulatory compliance.

Whether advising startups or multinational corporations, we help businesses navigate complex legal structures, manage risk, and seize opportunities for growth.

Intellectual Property Law

Protecting creativity and innovation is at the heart of our Intellectual Property (IP) Law practice. We specialize in securing and enforcing trademarks, patents, copyrights, and trade secrets for individuals, startups, and large corporations.

Our IP lawyers help clients safeguard their intellectual assets, defend against infringement, and navigate licensing agreements. We also offer comprehensive advice on managing and monetizing IP portfolios to maximize their commercial potential.

Conveyancing Law and Practice

At Ruth.K & Co. Advocates, our Conveyancing Law and Practice services ensure seamless transactions in property matters.

Whether buying, selling, or transferring real estate, we guide clients through the legal intricacies of property ownership, title transfers, and mortgages. We ensure compliance with all legal requirements while protecting the interests of our clients.

Pro Bono Services

At Ruth.K & Co. Advocates, we are committed to giving back to the community through our Pro Bono program. We provide free legal services to individuals and organizations who cannot afford representation, ensuring access to justice for marginalized and vulnerable populations.

Our attorneys volunteer their time and expertise to causes that matter, from human rights advocacy to nonprofit assistance, because we believe in the power of the law to create positive societal change.
